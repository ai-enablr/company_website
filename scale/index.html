The Problem

Speed got you here.
These patterns will stop you.

Moving fast optimises for shipping, not for scaling. Here's what we find in almost every rapidly-built codebase we review.

Architecture

No separation of concerns

Business logic, database calls, and API routes tangled together. Impossible to test, risky to change, painful to scale.

Security

Authentication gaps

Ad-hoc auth bolted on after the fact. Missing rate limiting, exposed endpoints, and secrets hardcoded in version control.

Performance

N+1 queries & no caching

Works fine at 100 users. Collapses at 10,000. No query optimisation, no cache layer, no load strategy in sight.

Reliability

Zero observability

No structured logging, no error tracking, no alerting. You find out something broke when a user emails you — or when it's on Reddit.

Maintainability

Undocumented everything

No API contracts, no onboarding docs, no explanation of why decisions were made. Every new hire is a liability for months.

Scalability

Monolithic deployments

One deploy touches everything. Can't scale individual services, can't do zero-downtime deploys, can't isolate failures.
