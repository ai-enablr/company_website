New Launch from AIEnablr

Meet WorkEnablr.
Your team’s operational intelligence layer.

Fixing your architecture is step one. The harder problem is keeping it healthy as your team grows. WorkEnablr connects to the tools your team already uses — Slack, Jira, Notion, GitHub — and surfaces bottlenecks, execution risks, and coordination failures before they become costly incidents or missed deadlines.

When a team scales past 10–15 people, coordination overhead starts compounding non-linearly. Decisions get lost in Slack threads. Ownership gets blurry. Standups stop surfacing the real blockers. WorkEnablr gives you visibility into all of it — automatically, without changing how your team works.

Bottleneck Detection
Identifies which people, processes, or systems are slowing your team down — before tickets pile up or launches slip.
Coordination Gap Analysis
Spots misalignments between teams — missed handoffs, unclear ownership, silent blockers — and surfaces them before they cascade.
Team Health Monitoring
Tracks review cycle times, deployment frequency, workload balance, and execution rhythm across engineering and product.
Deep Tool Integrations
Connects to Slack, Jira, Notion, Linear, and GitHub. No new workflow to adopt — just intelligence on top of what already exists.
